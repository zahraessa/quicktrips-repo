HCI

Brief

When we initially received our task we had a meeting to do some research on similar projects and the tools available for developing our project.

We initially decided the best approach to the problem would be to create a phone application which we based our initial HCI on.

Nonetheless, after some further thinking we decided that designing a website would be a better approach as it would give users access to our product on both their computers and mobile devices.

This website will be based on responsive design to ensure that it’s UI remains intuitive and functioning on all devices.

The rest of features remain the same as our initial design.

We felt that this change was also beneficial as many people feel more comfortable booking holidays on their computer over their phones in terms of entering booking information and payment methods.
